What is HATCH?

HATCH is a school-focused vertical under hackability. It partners with schools to provide entrepreneurship and innovation education for students in Grades 3–12. Through experiential learning, students explore ideas, solve real-world problems, build solutions, and develop entrepreneurial thinking.

HATCH combines creativity, innovation, technology, and hands-on application to create meaningful learning experiences beyond traditional classrooms.

Why Hatch?

Building future-ready capabilities through experiential learning.

Innovation & Problem Solving

Real-World Application
